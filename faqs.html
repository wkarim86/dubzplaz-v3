I haven't received my in-app purchase

Sometimes a situation can arise when a player spends money on a purchase, but resources aren't added to their account. This is extremely rare, but it is possible. In this case, please, wait a little while (it can take from a few minutes to a day).

If the resources still haven't been added, please submit a ticket to the technical support team directly from the game (Settings - Support).

Can I refund my In-App Purchase?

Android:

Please, submit a ticket to the technical support team directly from the game. Describe the situation as accurately as possible, and also attach a screenshot with the details of the payment you are experiencing issues with. The screenshot should clearly show the Transaction ID of the payment and the price.

To view the full history of your payments open the Google Payments Center.

IOS:

We kindly inform you, that payments cleared via AppStore are processed exclusively by Apple.
Please contact Apple Support Service for a more detailed investigation into your issue.

I was double-charged

Please, submit a ticket to the technical support team directly from the game (Settings - Help).

Billing issue not listed

If you have not found your problem in the list, please click on the button below to contact game support. Describe the situation as accurately as possible, and also attach a screenshot with the details of the payment you are experiencing issues with. The screenshot should clearly show the Transaction ID of the payment and the price.

**To view the full history of your payments:

Android - Open Google Payments Center (https://payments.google.com/)

iOS - From the iTunes menu, select iTunes Store -> Account -> View Account -> Purchase History -> See All
